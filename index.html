Privacy Policy Support

Effective Date: March 1, 2026

This Privacy Policy applies to mobile applications developed and published by iDroid App ("we", "our", or "us") for iOS and Android devices.

1. Information We Do Not Collect

Our applications do not collect personally identifiable information (PII) such as your name, email address, phone number, physical address, or account credentials. No account registration is required.

2. Advertising & Third-Party Services

To keep our applications free, we may display advertisements using Google AdMob (Google LLC).

AdMob may automatically collect:

  • Advertising Identifiers (IDFA on iOS, AAID on Android)
  • Device information (model, OS version, language)
  • Ad interaction data (impressions, clicks)

This information is used for:

  • Ad personalization (if permitted by user)
  • Frequency capping
  • Ad performance analytics

For more details, please review Google’s Privacy Policy:

https://policies.google.com/privacy

3. App Tracking Transparency (iOS)

On iOS devices, we use Apple’s App Tracking Transparency framework. We request permission before accessing the advertising identifier (IDFA). If permission is denied, personalized ads are disabled.

4. Android Advertising ID

On Android devices, advertising identifiers are used in compliance with Google Play Developer Program Policies. Users may reset or delete their advertising identifier in device settings.

5. Children's Privacy

Our applications are not directed to children under 13 years of age (or 16 in certain jurisdictions). We do not knowingly collect personal data from children.

6. Data Security

We rely on trusted third-party service providers that implement appropriate security measures to protect collected data.

7. Your Privacy Choices

You may disable personalized ads, reset your advertising identifier, or limit ad tracking through your device settings.

8. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Updates will be reflected by revising the effective date at the top of this page.
