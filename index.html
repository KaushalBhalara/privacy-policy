Privacy Policy

Effective Date: March 1, 2026

This Privacy Policy applies to mobile applications developed and published by iDroid App ("we", "our", or "us") for iOS and Android devices.

1. Information We Do Not Collect

Our applications do not collect, store, or request personally identifiable information (PII), including your name, email address, phone number, physical address, or account credentials. No account registration is required.

2. Advertising & Third-Party Services

To keep our applications free, we may display advertisements using Google AdMob (Google LLC).

AdMob may automatically collect:

  • Advertising Identifiers (IDFA on iOS, AAID on Android)
  • Device information (model, OS version, language)
  • Ad interaction data (impressions, clicks)

This data is used for ad personalization (if permitted), frequency capping, and analytics.

For more information, please review Google’s Privacy Policy:

https://policies.google.com/privacy

3. App Tracking Transparency (iOS)

On iOS devices, we use Apple’s App Tracking Transparency framework. We request permission before accessing the advertising identifier (IDFA). If permission is denied, personalized ads are disabled.

4. Android Advertising ID

On Android, advertising identifiers are used in compliance with Google Play policies. Users may reset or delete their advertising identifier in device settings.

5. Children's Privacy

Our applications are not directed to children under 13 (or 16 in certain jurisdictions). We do not knowingly collect personal data from children.

6. Data Security

We rely on trusted third-party providers that implement appropriate security measures to protect collected data.

7. Your Privacy Choices

You may disable personalized ads, reset your advertising identifier, or limit ad tracking through your device settings.

8. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Updates will be reflected by revising the effective date above.
